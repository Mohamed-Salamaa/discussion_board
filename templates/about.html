About Discussion Board

Welcome to Discussion Board — a simple, powerful platform built to bring people together for meaningful conversations. Our app helps communities, teams, and individuals create topic-focused boards where members can post questions, share insights, and engage in threaded discussions.

Why we built it

We believe that open, well-organized discussion is one of the best ways to learn and collaborate. Discussion Board makes it easy to structure conversations so knowledge is discoverable and every voice can contribute.

Key features
  • Boards Organize conversations by topic so people can find relevant discussions quickly.
  • Threaded Posts Keep replies grouped and readable with threaded comment flows.
  • Responsive Works great on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Lightweight Fast, minimal UI that focuses on content and conversation.
Who it's for

Whether you’re a student seeking help, an engineer brainstorming solutions, or a community organizer building discussion hubs — Discussion Board is designed to make collaboration straightforward and productive.

Get involved

Join a board, start a discussion, or invite your group — every conversation builds value. If you’d like help installing or customizing the app, check the project README or contact the maintainer.
